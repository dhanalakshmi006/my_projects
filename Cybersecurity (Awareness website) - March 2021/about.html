About us

We live in an era where everything is connected to the internet including our daily devices.

We save all our data on computers and other inter-connected devices. Cyber attacks are unwelcome attempts to steal, alter, or destroy this data through unauthorized access to our computer systems. And, cyber awareness is the first step towards cyber security.

My goal is to spread awareness and provide information that can help to secure our digital environment, and devices and protect the confidentiality, integrity, availability of our information and information assets.
